DIY Face Masks

Description of the Needs

We’ve all seen the lines and panic buying in response to the COVID-19 Pandemic. Toilet paper shortages may be making the headlines, but personal protective equipment (PPE), like medical face masks, are in extremely short supply and are vitally needed.

Cloth Face Masks

Cloth protective masks can be inexpensive and easy to make, even for folks with only basic sewing skills. This page provides links to some instructions and more importantly, attempts to provide a few mechanisms for donating what you’ve made to folks who don’t have the option of quarantining themselves.

3D Printed Face Shields

If you have a 3D printer, local hospitals and care facilities are in dire need of face shields, like the one here. We are still working through the delivery mechanism, but please let us know if you can assist.

We are all managing our COVID-19 response in our own ways. We urge you to find some way to help your community.
